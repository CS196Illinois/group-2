Features

Navigate Through Your Courses Easily

AllSchool provides a simpler way to access your course resources. Gone are the days of navigating between countless different websites to complete all your coursework. AllSchool centralizes them into an easy-to-use hub so that you can do what you need to do without the added hassle.


Collaborate With Your Peers

Do you know a resource that can help your classmates? Share it with them! The links for each course can be contributed to by students and course staff to add more resources for your benefit.


Organize Your Courses

We Realize That Organizing Your Courses Is A Hassle

Given the abundance of websites and online resources, as well as the recent need for remote learning, many educational institutions are employing multiple platforms for education. However, keeping track of these different websites can be difficult for both students and teachers.
