Features

Navigate Through Your Courses Easily

AllSchool provides a simpler way to access your course resources. Gone are the days of navigating between countless different websites to complete all your coursework, and missing half of it anyway: AllSchool centralizes them into an easy-to-use hub that you can do what you need to do without the added hassle.


Collaborate With Your Peers

Do you know a resource that can help your classmates? Share it with the other people in the course! The Extra Resources section for each course allows students and course staff to add more resources to make your learning experience more centralized.


Organize Your Courses

We Realize That Organizing Your Courses Is A Hassle

Given the abundance of useful online resources and the recent need for remote learning, many educational institutions are employing multiple platforms for education. However, keeping track of these different websites can be difficult for both students and teachers. With AllSchool, students have a hub to access cross-platform materials and resources-truly possessing a space for all school.
